Welcome to Shagun Banquet Hall

Shagun Banquet Hall in Uttarpara, West Bengal has established itself as a premier event venue in the region, offering a sophisticated blend of elegant ambiance and comprehensive services. Located near the Uttarpara Telephone Exchange, our venue provides the perfect setting for weddings, receptions, corporate events, and special occasions with capacity for 150-500 guests.
