Booking Process

Learn about our straightforward booking process to secure your perfect event date at Shagun Banquet Hall.

1

Initial Inquiry

Begin by submitting an inquiry through our booking form, calling us directly, or visiting our venue during business hours. Our team will check availability for your preferred date and provide initial information about our packages and services.

  • Provide event date, type, and approximate guest count
  • Review initial availability and options
  • Receive preliminary pricing information
2

Venue Visit

Schedule a visit to our banquet hall to see our spaces in person. Our event coordinator will guide you through the venue, discuss your event requirements in detail, and answer any questions you may have.

  • Tour our main banquet hall and additional spaces
  • Discuss your vision and specific requirements
  • Explore catering menus and decoration options
3

Booking Confirmation

Once you've decided to reserve your date, we require a 25% advance payment to secure your booking. This payment is non-refundable but can be adjusted within a six-month period for any occasion if your plans change.

  • Submit 25% advance payment to secure your date
  • Receive booking confirmation and receipt
  • Sign event agreement outlining all terms and services
4

Event Planning

Our experienced team will work with you to plan all aspects of your event. From menu selection to decoration themes, we'll ensure everything is tailored to your preferences. Planning meetings can be scheduled at your convenience.

  • Finalize menu selections and food requirements
  • Choose decoration themes and layouts
  • Discuss entertainment and technical requirements
  • Coordinate with our panel-approved vendors
5

Final Payment

The remaining balance payment is due seven days before your scheduled event date. This allows us adequate time for final preparations to ensure your event runs smoothly. All charges are subject to GST as per Indian regulations.

  • Complete remaining payment (including GST)
  • Confirm final guest count and arrangements
  • Receive detailed event execution plan
  • Relax and look forward to your special day!

Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend booking our venue at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for weekend dates and peak wedding seasons (October-February). For special dates or holiday periods, booking 6-12 months ahead is advisable to ensure availability.

We maintain a strict in-house catering policy and do not permit outside food vendors to ensure consistent quality control. Similarly, for decorations, we work exclusively with our panel-approved decorators starting at Rs. 20,000. This ensures high standards and aesthetic alignment with our venue.
