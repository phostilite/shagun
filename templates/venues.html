Venues Overview

Shagun Banquet Hall features three distinct event spaces, providing flexibility for different event sizes and formats to meet diverse client needs. Our venue can comfortably accommodate between 150 and 500 guests, making it suitable for both intimate gatherings and larger celebrations.

Each space showcases premium aesthetic elements including high ceilings, crystal chandeliers, and quality furnishings that contribute to an atmosphere of sophistication and luxury appropriate for special occasions.

Main Banquet Hall

Our grandest space showcases impressive capacity, comfortably accommodating between 150 and 400 guests for a wide range of events. The interior features high ceilings complemented by crystal chandeliers that create an atmosphere of grandeur appropriate for milestone celebrations.

Premium furnishings throughout the venue reinforce perceptions of quality and attention to detail. The hall's sophisticated lighting design enhances both the aesthetic appeal and functional aspects of events, with options that can be adjusted to create different moods from vibrant celebrations to intimate gatherings.

Secondary Space

Intimate Space

Complimentary Rooms

These well-appointed rooms feature comfortable furnishings, vanity areas, and adequate space to accommodate personal items. Particularly valuable for wedding parties, these rooms offer a quiet retreat from the main celebration while remaining conveniently accessible.
