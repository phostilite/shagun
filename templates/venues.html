Venues Overview

Shagun Banquet Hall features two magnificent main halls across two floors, offering a total of 8,000 sq. ft. of premium event space. Our venue can accommodate between 300 and 800 guests when using both spaces, making it perfect for grand celebrations and large-scale events.

Each hall boasts 4,000 sq. ft. of carpet area with attached balconies, providing versatile spaces for different event activities. The Main Hall on the 1st floor features a larger balcony ideal for Hi-Tea setups, while the Dining Hall on the 2nd floor offers elevated dining experiences with enhanced views.

Main Hall - 1st Floor

Our premier Main Hall on the 1st floor features 4,000 sq. ft. of carpet area, providing an elegant and spacious venue for grand celebrations. The hall boasts high ceilings with stunning crystal chandeliers that create an atmosphere of grandeur.

This magnificent space includes an attached larger balcony, perfect for Hi-Tea setups and cocktail arrangements. The hall features a strategically placed wash basin on the balcony side for convenience, plus bonus usable space that adds versatility to your event planning.

Dining Hall - 2nd Floor
