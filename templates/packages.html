Booking Terms

Reservation Process

Bookings require a 25% advance payment to secure your date. The remaining balance is due seven days before your event. All payments are non-refundable but may be adjusted for rescheduling within a six-month period.

Catering Policy

We maintain an in-house catering policy to ensure quality and consistency. Outside food vendors are not permitted. Our culinary team can accommodate special dietary requirements with advance notice.

Decoration Guidelines

All decorations must be arranged through our panel-approved decorators. This ensures quality and compatibility with our venue's aesthetics while allowing for personalized themes.

Alcohol Policy

If you wish to serve alcoholic beverages at your event, you must obtain the requisite liquor license in compliance with local regulations. Our team can provide guidance on this process.

Timing & Extensions

Standard venue rental is for the duration specified in your package. Extensions may be available at an additional hourly rate, subject to availability and prior arrangement.
